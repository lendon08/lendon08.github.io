FAQs
  • What is Factify? Factify is a fake news detector app that helps you verify the authenticity and reliability of any news article. Factify uses artificial intelligence and natural language processing to analyze the content, the source, the author, and the date of the article and provide you with a comprehensive report on its credibility. Factify also gives you a Factify Score, a numerical rating from 0 to 100 that indicates how trustworthy the article is.
  • How does Factify work? Factify works by comparing the news article with a large database of verified and unverified news sources, as well as with other articles on the same topic from different perspectives. Factify also checks the facts and claims made in the article against reliable sources of information, such as official statistics, scientific studies, and reputable media outlets. Factify then generates a report that summarizes the findings and shows you the evidence and references that support or contradict the article.
  • How can I use Factify? The Factify application allows you to copy and paste any news article in Filipino language. Just click on the Detect button and wait fo the results.
